AI Already Makes Decisions Like Humans

AI today plays a major role in decision making. From YouTube recommendations to self-driving cars, AI analyzes vast amounts of data and makes decisions just like us!

When Pat noticed all the recommended videos were about AI, it felt like YouTube was reading his mind. But it wasn't psychic. It just tracked his digital footprints like watch history and searches. This personalized experience is powered by algorithms, not emotions.

AI already helps with daily decisions, from food orders to job applications. But should we trust it for all important decisions?
