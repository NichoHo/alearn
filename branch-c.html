AI Can Make Decisions, But It Needs Human Guidance

AI can process data, spot patterns, and predict outcomes, but it still needs human input. When Alex asked whether to invest in Bitcoin, the AI replied with trends and risks, but didn’t make the decision for him.

That’s because AI provides information, not guarantees. It’s not a fortune teller, it’s a tool. The final call is up to the human using it.

To use AI effectively, we must combine its analytical power with our own judgment, ethics, and understanding of context.
