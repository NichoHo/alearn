AI Still Can’t Make Decisions Like Humans

Despite its advancements, AI lacks common sense, emotions, and human intuition. Jamie asked AI what to eat, pizza or sushi, and got unhelpful neutral responses instead of a straight answer.

This reflects a deeper issue: AI doesn’t actually “understand” like a human. It gives suggestions based on trends and probabilities, but not personal experience or emotions.

AI predicts patterns but doesn’t possess logic or empathy. Can it ever develop true understanding or human-like judgment?

Key Points

AI Lacks Common Sense

AI can’t provide simple, clear decisions because it doesn't truly "understand" your intentions or emotions.

Pattern Matching, Not Reasoning

AI answers are based on trends and probabilities, not real understanding or empathy.

User Frustration

When AI fails to respond like a human, it leads to frustration, proving it still has a long way to go.
